Hong Kong and Macao: handling Hong Kong and Macao residents' permits to and from the mainland

Hongkong residents can go to China Travel Agency in Hongkong. Applicants are required to submit application forms, pure white background color photos, and a positive copy of Hongkong identity documents. With the effective "Hong Kong and Macao residents to the mainland passport" (return home card) and need to change the new certificate, you can also choose to buy special launch envelopes in the Hongkong China Travel Agency door market, and put the application documents in the delivery box of the certificate door. Macao residents must go to Macao China Travel Agency, located in Nam Kwong building, Dr Roy ridge.
