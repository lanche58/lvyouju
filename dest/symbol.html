Sichuan symbol

hot pot

Chafing dish is the most distinctive food in Sichuan. It is an outstanding representative of Sichuan's catering culture.

01

There are many varieties of hot pot in Sichuan, including hot pot of tripe, yellow throat hot pot, duck intestine hot pot, yellow braised fish hot pot, fat sausage chafing pot, fish head hot pot, konjac duck hot pot, fusier side fish hot pot and so on. The rinsing material in the pot is even more numerous. Almost anything that can be eaten can be boiled, boiled and ironed. In recent years, on the basis of the traditional hot pot, the people of Sichuan have derived a series of strings (traditional string and cold pot string), and the "hot and hot" wind has quickly swept the whole of China.

Sichuan symbol

Panda

Sichuan is known as "the hometown of the giant panda".

02

The rare animal giant panda known as the "national treasure", 85% is inhabited by the mountains in the northwest of Sichuan. Sichuan is therefore known as "the home of the giant panda".


The lovely giant panda is one of the most precious rare animals in the world. It is the symbol of the World Wildlife Conservation Foundation. There are 18 giant panda natural reserves in China, 16 in Sichuan, and a "breeding base for giant pandas" in Northeast Chengdu, and Wolong Giant Panda Nature Reserve, 136 kilometers from Chengdu, is one of the three nature reserves in China. The World Wildlife Conservation Foundation has a "Giant Panda Research Center" and more than 300 from Chengdu. The Baoxing village of Baoxing is the first place in the world to discover giant pandas.

Sichuan Opera and
face change

Sichuan Opera is a major feature of Sichuan culture. Chengdu is the hometown of drama.

04

Sichuan Opera is a major feature of Sichuan culture. Chengdu is the hometown of drama. As early as the Tang Dynasty, there was "Shu opera crowning the world". In the Qing Dynasty, on the basis of local car lights, the Qing Dynasty absorbed the sound of the Suzhou, Jiangxi, Anhui, Hubei, Shaanxi and Gansu, and formed the "Sichuan Opera" in Sichuan dialect, which contains five kinds of vocal cavities, such as high cavity, Hu Qin, kungfu, lantern Opera, and play.


Sichuan opera has high tune, rich melody, beautiful singing and most local characteristics. It is the main form of singing in Sichuan Opera. The help of Sichuan Opera plays a leading role in the combination of chamber, chorus, accompaniment and chorus. The language of Sichuan Opera is lively, humorous, full of distinctive local colors, strong breath of life and a broad mass base. The famous opera of Sichuan Opera, the legend of the White Snake, is widely spread both at home and abroad.



Face change is one of the special techniques of the performing art of Sichuan Opera. The audience is very interested in it. After seeing it, it is a kind of romantic technique to reveal the inner thoughts and feelings of the characters in the play.
