Geography

About Sichuan

Hundreds of millions of years of geological movement, great changes have created a magnificent and steep Bashu scenery.

01

Sichuan is located in the hinterland of Southwest China and the upper reaches of the Yangtze River. Its western part is the Qinghai Tibet Plateau, the eastern part is the Sichuan basin, the geological movement of hundreds of millions of years, great changes in the vicissitudes, has created the magnificent and steep Bashu scenery. Sichuan, which is in the corridor of the blend of Chinese and Western countries and the transition between the north and the south, has been advantageous for years to combine and assimilate the East and the west, and also the key to cultural exchanges between the north and the south. The essence of civilization of the two great rivers and rivers of the Yangtze River and the Yellow River has nurtured the great and fantastic Bashu culture. Sichuan now has an area of 486 thousand square kilometers. By the end of 9 2016, the province has a permanent population of 91 million. It has 21 cities (States) and 182 counties (cities and districts).


The terrain of Sichuan is roughly divided into two distinct worlds along the East meridian 130 Meridian: the East is the famous Sichuan basin, the terrain is low, the hills and gullies spread between them, and the west is the plateau and mountain range, the high ground, the sky high clouds, the peak depth gorge, the layers, the snow mountains, the glaciers, the plateau, swamps, and prairies. ... There are many kinds of terrain in this area. Such landform conditions create different natural environments of Sichuan type and make Sichuan's tourism environment very advantageous.
